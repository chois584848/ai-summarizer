HORIZON RESEARCH INSTITUTE

The State of Remote Work 2026

Annual Industry Report · Published Jun 2026 · 28 pages

Executive Summary

Remote and hybrid work have settled into a durable equilibrium. After years of rapid change, most organizations have converged on flexible models that balance focus time with in-person collaboration.

64%run hybrid schedules
58%stable / higher output
41%cite collaboration gaps

Key Findings

  • Hybrid is now the default operating model for most teams.
  • Productivity held steady or improved for a majority of respondents.
  • Collaboration and onboarding are the most-cited friction points.

Methodology

Findings are based on a survey of 2,400 companies across 18 countries, combined with structured interviews with people leaders.

Outlook

Spending on asynchronous collaboration tools is projected to grow 30% year over year, and hybrid is expected to remain dominant through 2027.
